Recipe: hard-boiled egg
Makes: 1 egg

Ingredients:
- eggs (1)
- salt

Instructions:
- Fill pot with cool water
- Salt the water
- Add egg(s) to water
- Boil water
- As soon as water reaches boiling, remove pot from heat and let cool for 10 minutes
- After 10 minutes have passed, remove eggs from hot water and place in cold/cool water to bring to an edible temperature
